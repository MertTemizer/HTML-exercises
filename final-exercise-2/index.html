Recipe: Mert Temizer

Serving: 4 People Preparation Time: 30 minutes Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients for Chocolate Cubes Recipe..

  • 350 gr dark chocolate
  • 150 ml cream
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 150 gr pistachios (Almonds or hazelnuts can also be used)

How to Make Chocolate Cubes?

  1. Melt the dark chocolate.
  2. Add cream and butter to the melted chocolate and mix well and place the pot on a counter.
  3. Crush the pistachios in the air a little and break them into large and small pieces.
  4. Add the crushed pistachios to the chocolate mixture and mix.
  5. Pour the chocolate you have prepared into an aluminum square tray if you have one, or into a small tray that you have placed greaseproof paper on, extending from the edges.
  6. Leave in the refrigerator for two hours.
  7. Remove the chocolate from the mold. Break it into square pieces.
